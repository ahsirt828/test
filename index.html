TOP DESTINATIONS

Mt. Pulag
One of the foremost adventure destinations in Northern Luzon-Mount Pulag is in many Filipino and international mountain climbers' must-do list or bucket list, if you will.

Bohol
Bohol is the tenth largest island in the Philippines. It is one of the bigger islands in 'the Visayas', the group of islands in the center of the Philippines

Batanes
Batanes is a scene of changing moods,sometimes balmy, sometimes placid and other times ominous. But itspostcard-worthy topography and the genuine hospitality of its people-- the Ivatan, make it such a tourist-friendly place

Samal Island
The island offers world class choices of unspoiled beaches and niches which are ideal refuge to escape the hectic and fast paced urban life.

Boracay
Boracay Island in the Philippines is paradise for certified beach and sun-worshippers around the world. Also known as Beach Capital of the Philippines.

Coron, Palawan
